Philadelphia Christian Young Adults

Group List
  • Our purpose is to help you find weekly groups, gatherings, and events for Christian young adults in Philadelphia and surrounding areas.
  • We believe Jesus is the Son of God. He took our place on the cross to atone for our sins and rose from the dead. Whoever believes in Him will have eternal life with Him.
  • We desire to spread the Gospel to all the world, to love others as Christ loves, to worship God with our brothers and sisters in Christ, and to study God's Word together.
